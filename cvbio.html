Curriculum Vitae

Matthieu (Donald Maurice) Hallé is a professional film/video artist and director living in Ottawa, ON. He was born  in Chilliwack, BC into a military family and has been very mobile all his life. He is mostly self-taught and briefly attended Concordia University’s Art History and Film Studies program in Montréal. He is the director of various short videos, as well as the feature length, Margraue (2013). He also performs visual shows, often improvised, and with the use of elements like water and fire.
