OCEAN SKETCH #1 (2017) - Super 8 - 2 mins - B&W - 1.36:1 - Silent

 

The film Ocean Sketch #1 was first recorded digitally on a beach in Zambujeira do Mar, Portugal, and then transferred to super8 film at my home in Ottawa. It was filmed in one take using a combination of free-lensing techniques, an Oldfield filter, and the play of automatic exposure settings.
