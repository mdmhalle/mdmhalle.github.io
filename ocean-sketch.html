OCEAN SKETCH (2017)

OCEAN SKETCH (2017) - Super 8mm - 2 mins - B&W - 1.36:1 - Silent

 

The film Ocean Sketch was first recorded digitally on a beach in Zambujeira do Mar, Portugal, and then transferred to super8 film at my home in Ottawa. It was filmed in one take using a combination of freelensing techniques, an Oldfield filter, and the play of automatic exposure settings.

 

Ocean Sketch is the first film in an ongoing series of 'travel sketches'.

Distributed by the CFMDC (Canadian Filmmakers Distribution Centre).
