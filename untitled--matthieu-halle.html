artist / filmmaker

I had a dream of a piano like instrument with lenses on the end of each hammer, that, when played, moved the attached lens into a beam of light and distorted the projected image.

 

Untitled (broken glass and fire), is a live and adaptive performance technique I developed to collaborate with musicians and create and improvise visual shows that feel, for me, hands-on, and very physical.

 

The imagery is created by holding broken pieces of crystal to focus candle light onto a video sensor that outputs directly to a digital video projector. My breath manipulates the flicker of the flame, and my hands control the movement and shape of the abstract light images.
