A live audio/visual performance collaboration between myself and musician Adam Saikaley commissioned by SAW video.

 

The piece is inspired by cymatics (the study of visible sound). For this performance, a tank of water with a mirrored surface is placed on top of a subwoofer playing the live music. Depending on the specific frequency, different nodal patterns emerge in the water. The video is projected through the water and reflected onto a screen resulting in a final image that is distorted by the ripple patterns.

 

This was performed August 1st, 2014 at St. Alban's church, Ottawa, ON.
