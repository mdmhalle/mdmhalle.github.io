Matthieu (Donald Maurice) Hallé is a video artist currently living in Ottawa, ON. He performs visuals, is the director of the feature film, Margraue (2013), and various shorts. He's mindful of his birth in Chilliwack, BC, being a nomad most of his life, studying film at Concordia University, and farming in the summers.
