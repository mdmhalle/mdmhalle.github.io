Synesthesia is a live audio/visual performance collaboration between myself and musician Adam Saikaley commissioned by SAW video.

 

The piece is inspired by cymatics (the study of visible sound). In this setup, a tank of water with a mirrored surface is placed on top of a sub-woofer. Depending on the specific frequencies playing, different nodal patterns emerge in the water. The video is projected through the water and reflected onto a screen resulting in a final image that is distorted by the ripple patterns in the water.

 

It premiered August 1, 2014 at St. Albans in Ottawa, ON with Adam Saikaley on no-input mixer.
