artist / filmmaker

Matthieu (Donald Maurice) Hallé is a video artist currently living in Ottawa, ON. He performs live visual shows, and is the director of various short videos, as well as the feature length, Margraue (2013). He is mindful of his birth in Chilliwack, BC, his nomadic upbringing, studying film in Montréal, and farming in the summers.
