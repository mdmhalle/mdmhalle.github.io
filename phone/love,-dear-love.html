Love, Dear Love is a multi-disciplinary collaboration with composer Rebecca Gray and visual artist Rachel Gray based on Shakespeare's Othello and King Lear.

 

It premiered July 23, 2016 at House of Common, Ottawa, ON with the following musicians:

 

sopranos: Susan Brown, Rebecca Gray

violin: Christina Deaville, Emma Gauthier-Mamaril

viola: Jaya Paquette-Jager

cello: Jaeyong Chong
